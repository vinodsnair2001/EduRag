# Student Guide

> [[_HOME|← Home]] · [[00-Getting-Started|← Getting Started]]

## Logging In

1. Open the EduRAG app: `http://localhost:3000`
2. Enter your email and password (given to you by your teacher)
3. You will land on the **Class Selection** screen

---

## Choosing Your Class and Subject

1. On the **Class Selection** screen you will see colourful cards for each class
2. Click your class (e.g., "Class 7")
3. You will see the subjects available
4. Click the subject you want to study (e.g., "Science")
5. The chat window opens, scoped to that class and subject

> You can always click **Back** to choose a different subject.

---

## Chatting with AI

Once in the chat window, you can ask anything from your study material.

### Asking a Question

1. Type your question in the box at the bottom
2. Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button
3. The AI will search the uploaded PDFs and answer based on the content

**Good questions:**
- "What is photosynthesis?"
- "Explain the water cycle in simple terms"
- "What are the differences between acids and bases?"
- "Summarize Chapter 3 — Newton's Laws"

**The AI only answers from uploaded study material.** If you ask about something not in the PDFs, it will say: *"I couldn't find this in the uploaded study material."*

### Reading AI Answers

- Answers appear as chat bubbles with the AI's avatar
- **Bold text** highlights key terms
- Lists and bullet points are formatted for easy reading
- You can scroll up to re-read earlier answers

---

## Practice Questions

To get practice questions, type one of these phrases:

- "Give me practice questions"
- "Quiz me on this topic"
- "Test me on Chapter 2"
- "Create 5 questions about photosynthesis"

The AI will generate **5 questions** based on the study material:
- 2 easy questions
- 2 medium questions
- 1 hard question

### Answering Practice Questions

1. A **Practice Card** appears for each question
2. Type your answer in the answer box
3. Click **Submit Answer**
4. The AI will tell you:
   - ✅ **CORRECT** — great job, concept reinforced
   - ⚡ **PARTIALLY CORRECT** — what you got right and what was missing
   - ❌ **INCORRECT** — gentle explanation of the correct answer
5. A star rating appears based on your performance

### Multiple Choice Questions

Some questions are multiple choice:
1. Click one of the options (A, B, C, D)
2. Click **Submit**
3. See if you're correct

## Tips for Better Answers

| What you want | How to ask |
|--------------|-----------|
| Simple explanation | "Explain [topic] in simple words" |
| Step-by-step | "Walk me through [process] step by step" |
| Examples | "Give me an example of [concept]" |
| Summary | "Summarize the main points about [topic]" |
| Comparison | "What is the difference between [A] and [B]?" |
| Practice | "Quiz me on [chapter/topic]" |

---

## Chat History

Your chat history is saved. When you return to the same class+subject chat, your previous conversation will still be there. You can scroll up to review earlier questions and answers.

To start fresh:
1. Go back to Class Selection
2. Return to the chat — a new session begins while history of past sessions remains

---

## What AI Can and Cannot Do

| Can do | Cannot do |
|--------|-----------|
| Answer questions from uploaded study material | Answer questions not in the PDFs |
| Generate practice questions | Replace your teacher |
| Grade your answers with explanations | Access the internet |
| Explain concepts at your grade level | See other students' chats |
